What are ETFs?

Exchange-Traded Funds (ETFs) are investment funds traded on stock exchanges, much like stocks. They hold assets such as stocks, commodities, or bonds and generally operate with an arbitrage mechanism designed to keep trading close to its net asset value.

Action Plan

  1. Research basic ETF types: Equity, Bond, Sector-specific.
  2. Identify TSX-listed ETFs like VCN (Canadian Equity).
